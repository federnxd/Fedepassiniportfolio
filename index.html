About Me

Hi, my name is Federico Passini, I am an Audio Post-Production and Production Specialist based in Rosario, Santa Fe, Argentina. With over five years of professional experience in the industry. My passion is creating sound experiences, bringing ideas to life through audio production and processing.

I hold advanced diplomas in Digital Audio and Audio for Video Games from Universidad Tecnológica Nacional de Buenos Aires (UTN.BA), a certification in Music Production and Recording from Berklee College of Music, and professional operation credentials from Fundación Latinoamericana de Audio Profesional (FUNLAP).

My expertise is further enhanced by specialized training in Sampling & Synthesis, Multiplatform Streaming, Digital Console Operation, and advanced Mixing & Mastering from institutions like CETEAR, Ocho records, etc. Furthermore I hold diplomas in Software - QA from Universidad Tecnológica Nacional - Facultad Regional Resistencia (UTN.FRR) and in library and archive assistant technician from UTF.

My Studio - "Mítica Studio"

I work from my own acoustically-designed studio, treated with specific panels, traps, and diffusers I built myself and measured with Room EQ Wizard. It is equipped with first-line microphones, a high-end mixing console, precision monitors and headphones, and a wide array of instruments and MIDI controllers. This ensures that every step, from recording to the final master, is executed in a critical listening environment.
